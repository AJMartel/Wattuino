ATmega32U4 Fuse Settings
------------------------
Extended: 0xCB
High:     0xD8
Low:      0xFF
Lockbits: 0xCF (LPM and SPM prohibited in Boot Section)


USB Vendor and Product ID
-------------------------
VID: 0x1D50 (Openmoko)
PID: 0x60B0 (http://wiki.openmoko.org/wiki/USB_Product_IDs)

VID 0x6666 is a prototype product Vendor ID
http://www.linux-usb.org/usb.ids

VID 0x1209 is for open-source projects
http://pid.codes
